# 角色特徵分析：Luca Paguro（Pixar 動畫《Luca》）

### A) 簡短核心描述（≤40詞）

12-year-old italian pre-teen boy, short and slim build, large round brown eyes, thick arched eyebrows, button red-tinted nose, rosy cheeks, soft oval face; short dark-brown wavy curls with front quiff; barefoot; pixar stylized skin with subtle SSS and smooth shading.

---

### B) 詳細特徵列表

**1) 年齡與體型（Age & Build）**

* pre-teen, ~12 years old; short stature; slim/lanky youthful build
* proportionally large head, small torso, thin arms and legs; delicate hands and feet; barefoot in all shots

**2) 臉部輪廓與五官（Face & Features）**

* face shape: soft oval with rounded jawline and gentle chin
* eyes: very large round eyes, warm medium-brown irises, wide set, thick dark limbal edge, pronounced upper eyelid crease
* eyebrows: thick, dark, arched; highly expressive (raised, knitted, determined)
* nose: small button nose with visible red tint on tip and bridge
* cheeks: noticeable rosy blush on both sides; slight freckling impression from shader grain (not strong spots)
* mouth/teeth: small mouth; seen open in surprise/fear, closed smile, grit-teeth “determined” look; upper incisors occasionally visible
* ears: rounded, slightly protruding, pink inner cartilage
* overall impression: innocent/curious baseline with quick shifts to anxious or determined expressions

**3) 頭髮（Hair）**

* color: dark brown/chestnut
* style: short to medium length wavy/curly; defined locks with sculpted loops; front quiff curling upward; tidy but naturally tousled
* sheen: soft specular highlights consistent with stylized PBR hair cards

**4) 皮膚與材質（Skin/Materials, 3D 渲染）**

* complexion: fair to lightly sun-kissed; warm undertone
* shading: pixar/semi-stylized skin, smooth micro-surface; soft subsurface scattering (ears, nose, cheeks)
* lighting response: warm rim light and soft diffuse fill; gentle bloom; anti-aliased silhouettes; shallow DOF in close-ups

**5) 服裝（Clothing）**

* **Look A – Leaf skirt**：green woven leaf skirt/belt with scalloped leaf edge; shirtless upper body; barefoot
* **Look B – Town outfit**：light sea-foam/stone-green short-sleeve button-up with subtle thin green plaid; rolled sleeves; small collar; short blue shorts（denim-like cotton）；barefoot
* buttons/stitches visible; natural cloth folds; soft fabric shader

**6) 姿勢、動作與表情（Poses, Actions, Expressions）**

* anxious clutch: both fists tucked under chin/chest, shoulders slightly hunched, worried mouth shape
* side run / mid-stride: profile view, arms pumping forward/back
* hands-on-hips “brave” stance: chest forward, elbows akimbo, grin with exposed teeth
* crouching curiosity: kneeling, one hand touching ground, gentle smile, head tilted down
* looking-up wonder: neck extended, eyes wide, mouth slightly open
* triumphant pump: one arm raised with fist, big hopeful smile
* lean-in focus: torso hinged forward, hands on knees, narrowed eyes
* tense confrontation: clenched fist, knitted brows, determined/pouting mouth

**7) 場景、光線與鏡頭（Scene/Lighting/Camera）**

* mediterranean outdoor ambience; warm late-afternoon key with soft sky fill; occasional back/rim light
* close-up to mid-shot coverage; shallow DOF in portraits, wider lens when showing full body
* camera angles include high-angle look-up, side profile tracking, frontal close-ups

**8) 風格標記（Style Cues）**

* pixar 3D animation style; semi-real PBR materials; smooth shading; subtle filmic bloom; gentle grain; saturated yet pastel seaside palette

**9) 明確避免（Disambiguation for training）**

* no sea-monster traits (no scales/fins/blue-green skin)
* not Alberto (avoid tanner skin + green tank), not Giulia (no beanie/striped orange tee), not Ercole (no pink sweater drape)
