# Ercole Visconti - Character Profile

**Film:** Luca (2021, Pixar)
**Role:** Main Antagonist
**Voice Actor:** Saverio Raimondo
**Species:** Human
**Age:** Approximately 18 years old (allegedly 16)
**Occupation:** Five-time Portorosso Cup Champion (self-proclaimed)

---

## Quick Reference

Ercole Visconti is the arrogant, narcissistic teenage bully of Portorosso who has won the town's annual Cup race five consecutive years. Despite believing everyone adores him, nearly the entire town despises him. He owns a shiny red Vespa and is followed everywhere by his two lackeys, Ciccio and Guido, whom he constantly mistreats.

---

## Background

### Status & Reputation
- **Five-time Portorosso Cup champion** (ages 13-17, approximately)
- **Self-proclaimed town hero** (delusional)
- **Actually hated by most townspeople:** children fear him, adults find him annoying
- **Even the local priest dislikes him**
- **"Allegedly 18 years old"** but claims to be 16 to stay eligible for race
- **Too old for the competition** but refuses to stop entering

### Social Circle
- **Followers:** Ciccio and Guido (eventually rebel against him)
- **Rivals:** Giulia Marcovaldo (his main target)
- **Enemies:** Luca, Alberto, and most of Portorosso

## Personality

### Surface Traits
- **Narcissistic:** Believes he's beloved and admired
- **Delusionally self-confident:** Thinks everyone respects him
- **Loud and theatrical:** Poses, preens, shows off constantly
- **Materialistic:** Obsessed with Vespa and wool sweater
- **Vain:** Cares deeply about appearance and status

### Dark Traits
- **Cruel bully:** Physically and verbally intimidates children
- **Obsessed with reputation:** Desperate to maintain winning streak
- **Greedy:** Motivated by prize money and rewards
- **Sociopathic tendencies:** Willing to murder Luca/Alberto for money
- **Cowardly:** Falls apart when confronted or abandoned
- **Cheater:** Uses olive oil, forces Guido to eat pasta, breaks rules

### Behavioral Patterns
- **Physical bullying:** Dunking, punching, intimidation
- **Verbal abuse:** Mocking, insulting, belittling
- **Bad boss:** Abuses Ciccio and Guido constantly
- **Immature:** Cries over wet sweater, throws tantrums
- **Overreacts emotionally:** Devastated by small setbacks

### Character Arc
**Beginning:** Arrogant town bully with inflated ego
**Middle:** Becomes obsessed with capturing sea monsters for reward
**End:** Humiliated, defeated, abandoned by followers, crying over ruined sweater

## Relationships

### Ciccio & Guido (Lackeys)
- **Devoted followers (initially):** Do everything for him
- **Constantly abused:** Smacked with sandwiches, forced to compete unfairly
- **Fear him:** Obey out of intimidation
- **Eventually rebel:** Throw him in fountain after final insult
- **Turning point:** "Be useful for once in your pathetic lives!"

### Giulia Marcovaldo (Arch-Nemesis)
- **Long-time rival:** She hates him openly
- **Bullies her relentlessly:** Calls her "Spewlia"
- **Dismisses her accomplishments:** Mocks her losses
- **She's determined to defeat him:** Main motivation for entering race

### Luca Paguro & Alberto Scorfano (Targets)
- **Immediate targets:** Kicks ball into his Vespa
- **Attempts to dunk Luca:** Fountain intimidation
- **Punches Alberto:** Physical violence
- **Tries to murder them:** Harpoon attacks when revealed as sea monsters
- **Motivated by reward money:** Greedy for prize

### Town of Portorosso
- **Children:** Fear and hate him
- **Adults:** Find him annoying, loud, disrespectful
- **Signora Marsigliese:** Tolerates him as participant
- **Don Eugenio (priest):** Dislikes him
- **Ultimately reject him:** Town sides with sea monsters over him

### Character Development
- **Originally would have been older friend of Giulia:** Changed to pure antagonist
- **Novelization detail:** Cannot swim (makes championship suspicious)
