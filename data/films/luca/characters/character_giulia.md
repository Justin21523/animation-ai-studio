# Giulia Marcovaldo - Character Profile

**Film:** Luca (2021, Pixar)
**Role:** Tritagonist
**Voice Actor:** Emma Berman
**Species:** Human
**Age:** Approximately 12-13 years old
**Birthday:** March 18

---

## Quick Reference

Giulia is an outgoing, curious Italian girl who spends summers in Portorosso with her father Massimo. During the school year, she lives in Genova with her mother. As a local outsider and frequent target of bully Ercole Visconti, she immediately befriends Luca and Alberto, forming "Team Underdogs" to win the Portorosso Cup.

---

## Background & Family

### Family Structure
- **Father:** Massimo Marcovaldo (lives in Portorosso, fisherman)
- **Mother:** Maria Marcovaldo (lives in Genova, artist)
- **Pets:** Machiavelli (cat, with father), Nerone (dog, with mother)
- **Parents separated** during her early childhood
- **Dual residence:** School year in Genova, summers in Portorosso

### Past Experiences
- **Portorosso Cup history:** Multiple losses, previous year lost due to vomiting from eating too much pasta
- **Nickname from Ercole:** "Spewlia" (mockery of her pasta-eating failure)
- **Social status:** Local outsider, hasn't cultivated many friendships due to seasonal presence
- **Motivation:** Desperate to win the Cup to prove herself and end Ercole's bullying

## Personality

### Core Traits
- **Outgoing and spirited:** Loud, expressive, enthusiastic
- **Intellectually curious:** Loves books, astronomy, learning
- **Brave and loyal:** Willing to sacrifice race victory to protect friends
- **Slightly tomboyish:** Energetic, athletic, playful behavior
- **Childlike and playful:** Pretends to play invisible trumpet, acts out scenarios
- **Empathetic:** Recognizes other outsiders, immediately offers alliance
- **Determined:** Won't give up on beating Ercole despite repeated losses
- **Open-minded:** Initially scared of sea monsters but quickly understands they're harmless

### Character Arc
**Beginning:** Lonely outsider determined to beat Ercole, works solo
**Middle:** Opens up to Luca and Alberto, shares her love of astronomy and learning
**End:** Values friendship over winning, risks life to protect friends, strengthened bonds

### Personality Quirks
- **Cheese exclamations:** "Gorgonzola!", "Ricotta!", "Mozzarella!" (used as exclamations)
- **Military wake-up call:** Pretends to play trumpet music in the morning
- **Passionate teaching:** Excitedly explains stars, planets, school to Luca
- **Impulsive protector:** Crashes bike into Ercole without hesitation

## Relationships

### Luca Paguro
- **Close friend, intellectual connection**
- Shares astronomy, books, knowledge with him
- Discovers he's a sea monster, urges him to leave for safety
- Protective, willing to sacrifice herself
- Helps him attend school in Genova

### Alberto Scorfano
- **Initially cautious, becomes close friend**
- Alberto sees her as threat at first (jealousy)
- Discovers his sea monster form, initially frightened
- Protects him during race finale
- Eventually part of their friend group

### Massimo Marcovaldo (Father)
- **Loving, supportive relationship**
- Stays with him during summers
- He worries about her entering race due to past humiliation
- He defends her friends when revealed as sea monsters
- Works together on fish deliveries

### Maria Marcovaldo (Mother)
- **Lives with her during school year in Genova**
- Mother is an artist, accepting of Luca as sea monster
- Shown in credits welcoming Luca to their home

### Ercole Visconti
- **Arch-nemesis, long-standing rival**
- He bullies her for being "weird" and different
- She calls him out publicly, insults him
- Determined to end his "reign of terror"
- Eventually defeats him with Luca and Alberto

## Story Role & Key Moments

### Meeting & Alliance
1. Rescues Luca and Alberto from Ercole's fountain dunking
2. Explains she's an "underdog" like them
3. Proposes forming team for Portorosso Cup
4. Names them "Team Underdogs"
5. Invites them to stay at her house

### Training & Bonding
6. Trains them for triathlon (swimming/biking/eating)
7. Takes Luca to telescope, shows him stars and Saturn
8. Gifts her astronomy book to Luca
9. Explains her school in Genova, inspires Luca's dream of education
10. Grows close to Luca through shared intellectual interests

### Conflict & Discovery
11. Alberto reveals sea monster form, she screams in fear
12. Discovers Luca is also a sea monster (water splash)
13. Accepts them but urges Luca to leave for his safety
14. Fears for their lives in sea-monster-hunting town

### Race & Sacrifice
15. Competes in Portorosso Cup alongside Luca (Alberto returns)
16. Helps Luca discreetly during competition
17. Crashes bike into Ercole to save Luca and Alberto from harpoon
18. Gets hurt but helped by Luca and Alberto
19. Celebrates victory as Underdogs win the Cup

### Resolution
20. Prepares to return to Genova for school
21. Alberto buys Luca train ticket to join her
22. Credits show Luca living with her and her mother
23. They stay connected through phone calls

## Trivia & Details

### Production Notes
- **Voice actress:** Emma Berman researched role by meeting Italian waiter, learned Italian phrases via Zoom
- **Name pronunciation:** "Giulia" pronounced like English "Julia"
- **Birthday:** March 18 (confirmed)
- **Earlier versions:** Originally slightly older (15-16), more acquainted with Ercole
- **Cut content:** Original version had her discovering sea monsters early, made hunt enthusiastic
- **Relationship with Luca:** Originally had romantic hints, changed to purely platonic

### Easter Eggs in Her Room
- **Paperino (Donald Duck) plush** on her shelf
- **4*Town record** (from Turning Red, adapted as "4*Villaggi" in Italian)

### Character Development
- Name previously used for deleted Cars 2 character
- Stomach clutch after pasta scene hints Ercole wasn't lying about her getting sick
- Her "weird" status reflects director's own childhood feelings
- Represents acceptance and intellectual curiosity themes
