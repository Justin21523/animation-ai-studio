# Alberto Scorfano - Character Profile

**Film:** Luca (2021, Pixar)
**Role:** Deuteragonist
**Voice Actor:** Jack Dylan Grazer
**Species:** Sea monster (transforms to human when dry)
**Age:** Approximately 14 years old

---

## Quick Reference

Alberto is a bold, adventurous sea monster boy who lives alone in an abandoned tower. He introduces Luca to the surface world, teaching him confidence through "Silenzio, Bruno!" Despite his tough exterior, Alberto struggles with abandonment issues and ultimately finds family with Massimo.

---

## Physical Appearance

### Human Form
- **Build:** Slightly more athletic/stocky than Luca, sun-tanned
- **Skin:** Tan/bronze skin tone
- **Hair:** Messy, dark brown curly hair
- **Eyes:** Confident, mischievous expression
- **Clothing:** Green vest (often shirtless underneath), rolled-up pants/shorts, barefoot
- **Features:** Rebellious energy, confident posture, cocky grin

### Sea Monster Form
- **Scales:** Orange-green/teal iridescent scales
- **Fins:** Sharper, triangular fins (stronger swimmer than Luca)
- **Eyes:** Similar to human form but with yellow sclera
- **Tail:** More robust, athletic caudal fins
- **Features:** Slightly more muscular build, wild appearance

## Personality

### Core Traits
- **Confident and bold:** Fearless, takes risks, teaches Luca courage
- **Free-spirited:** Lives by his own rules, values freedom
- **Protective:** Fiercely loyal to Luca, willing to sacrifice
- **Impulsive:** Acts before thinking, sometimes reckless
- **Vulnerable beneath:** Hides deep abandonment trauma
- **Mischievous troublemaker:** Playful, rebellious, "Silenzio Bruno" attitude

### Character Arc
**Beginning:** Independent loner pretending to be confident, hiding abandonment
**Middle:** Best friend and mentor to Luca, struggles with jealousy over Giulia
**End:** Learns to accept help, finds family with Massimo, lets Luca go to school

### Signature Phrase
**"Silenzio, Bruno!"** - His philosophy for overcoming fear and self-doubt

---

## Relationships

### Luca Paguro
- **Best friend, brother-like bond**
- Teaches Luca to be brave and free
- Experiences jealousy but ultimately sacrifices for Luca's future
- Most important relationship in his life

### Massimo Marcovaldo
- **Father figure, adoptive family**
- Gradually builds trust and belonging
- Learns fishing, responsibility, and stability
- Finds the home he never had

### Giulia Marcovaldo
- **Initially jealous rival, later friend**
- Sees her as threat to his bond with Luca
- Eventually accepts her as part of their circle

### Alberto's Father
- **Abandoned Alberto years ago**
- Left 383 tally marks counting days
- Deep source of Alberto's emotional wounds
- Never returns

## Story Role Summary

**Key Moments** (not full plot):
1. Collects human objects underwater, pranks Luca
2. Pulls Luca to surface, teaches him to walk
3. Shows hideout filled with collected treasures
4. Teaches "Silenzio, Bruno!" philosophy
5. Builds DIY Vespas with Luca
6. Grows jealous of Luca and Giulia's friendship
7. Reveals sea monster form to prove a point
8. Feels abandoned when Luca betrays him
9. Returns with umbrella to help Luca in rain
10. Sells Vespa to buy Luca train ticket, stays with Massimo

### Primary Goals
- Maintain freedom and independence
- Keep Luca as his best friend
- Own a real Vespa and travel the world

### Hidden Desires

- Freedom vs. connection
- Self-reliance vs. accepting help

## Backstory & Trauma

### Abandonment Timeline
- Father left Alberto alone on the island
- Alberto counted 383 days before stopping
- Convinced himself "he's better off without me"
- Developed independent survival skills
- Hides vulnerability with bravado

### Coping Mechanisms
- **"Silenzio, Bruno!"** philosophy (suppress fear/pain)
- Pretends not to need anyone
- Becomes the teacher/leader role
- Collects objects to fill emptiness
- Acts confident to hide insecurity

### Healing Arc
- Massimo provides stable father figure
- Learns it's okay to accept help
- Finds belonging without losing self
- Realizes friendship can include others

## Trivia & Notes

- **Namesake:** Based on director's childhood friend Alberto Surace
- **Voice actor:** Alberto Surace voices a fisherman in Italian dub
- **Physical design:** Represents "stronger swimmer" (sharper fins)
- **Age:** Slightly older than Luca, more worldly
- **Lies about dad:** Claims his dad teaches him everything
- **Tower:** Filled with collected "land monster stuff"
- **Vest:** Rarely removes his signature green vest
- **Barefoot:** Almost always without shoes

### Character Symbolism
- Vespa = freedom and escape
- Tower = isolation and self-reliance
- Umbrella = sacrifice and protection
- "Silenzio Bruno" = internal growth
