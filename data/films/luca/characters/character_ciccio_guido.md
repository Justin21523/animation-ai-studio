# Ciccio & Guido - Character Profile

**Film:** Luca (2021, Pixar)
**Role:** Secondary Antagonists → Supporting Characters
**Voice Actors:** 
- **Ciccio:** Peter Sohn
- **Guido:** Lorenzo Crisci
**Species:** Human
**Age:** Teenage boys (approximately 13-15)

---

## Quick Reference

Ciccio and Guido are two boys who serve as Ercole Visconti's loyal henchmen and followers. They spend most of the film doing whatever they can to please Ercole—carrying his sandwiches, protecting his Vespa, and assisting in bullying—despite constant abuse. By the film's end, they finally stand up to him and push him into the fountain, ending his reign.

---

## Character Distinctions

### Ciccio (The More Devoted One)
- **Build:** Chubby, rounder face
- **Personality:** More naïve, eager to please, throws himself into harm's way
- **Loyalty:** Blindly devoted, catches falling Vespa without hesitation
- **Behavior:** More physical (does the swimming portion of race)

### Guido (The More Aware One)
- **Build:** Slim, lankier
- **Personality:** Slightly more hesitant, shows moral boundaries
- **Awareness:** Occasionally resists (stops boat from hitting boys)
- **Humor:** Snickers at "catfish" insult, gets smacked for it
- **Behavior:** Shows some independent thought

## Personality

### Shared Traits
- **Cooperative and obedient:** Follow every order from Ercole
- **Look up to Ercole:** (Initially) admire and want to impress him
- **Willing to harm innocents:** Assist in bullying at Ercole's command
- **Oblivious to abuse:** Don't recognize mistreatment until breaking point
- **Reluctant to hurt each other:** Uncomfortable when forced to slap each other
- **Eventually self-aware:** Recognize Ercole's true nature and rebel

### Ciccio's Personality
- **More devoted:** Throws himself into danger without question
- **Naïve follower:** Doesn't question Ercole's orders
- **Physical devotion:** Catches Vespa, swims with oil, endures abuse
- **Loyal to a fault:** Slower to recognize mistreatment

### Guido's Personality
- **More cautious:** Shows slight hesitation at cruel acts
- **Moral compass:** Stops boat from running over boys
- **Sense of humor:** Can't help laughing at "catfish" insult
- **Slightly independent:** Questions orders occasionally
- **First to show resistance:** More aware of Ercole's abuse

### Character Arc
**Beginning:** Devoted followers, help Ercole bully others
**Middle:** Continue obeying despite increasing abuse and humiliation
**End:** Finally recognize Ercole's cruelty, rebel and throw him in fountain

## Relationships

### Ercole Visconti (Boss/Abuser)
- **Initially worship him:** Do everything to make him happy
- **Fear him:** Obey out of intimidation
- **Constantly abused:**
  - Smacked with sandwiches
  - Insulted and berated
  - Forced to compete unfairly
  - Made to slap each other as punishment
  - Called "idiots" and "pathetic"
- **Breaking point:** "Be useful for once in your pathetic lives!"
- **Rebellion:** Push him and his sweater into fountain

### Luca Paguro & Alberto Scorfano
- **Initially enemies:** Assist Ercole in bullying them
- **Hunt them as sea monsters:** Armed with harpoons
- **Eventually neutral:** No longer pursue after Ercole's defeat

### Giulia Marcovaldo
- **Assist in bullying her:** Part of Ercole's harassment
- **Steal her entry money:** Under Ercole's orders
- **Eventually non-hostile:** No interaction after rebellion

### Each Other
- **Cooperative partnership:** Work together as team
- **Reluctant to hurt each other:** Discomfort when forced to slap
- **United in rebellion:** Act together to throw Ercole in fountain

## Punishment & Breaking Point

### Forced Slapping Scene
- **Guido's disobedience:** Stops boat from hitting Luca and Alberto
- **Ercole's punishment:** Makes them slap each other
- **Their discomfort:** Reluctant to hurt each other
- **Significance:** Shows they have more humanity than Ercole

### Pattern of Abuse
- Physical: Used as swimming dummy, pasta-eating tool
- Verbal: Constant insults, called idiots
- Emotional: No appreciation, only criticism
- Social: Humiliated in front of town during race

### The Final Straw
**Ercole's words:** "Give me another harpoon, idiots! Be useful for once in your pathetic lives!"
**Their realization:** They've endured enough abuse
**Their action:** United rebellion, push him into fountain
**Result:** Freedom from his tyranny

## Character Development

### Throughout Film
- **Early:** Eager to please, don't recognize abuse
- **Middle:** Increasingly uncomfortable but still obedient
- **Late:** Beginning to question, subtle resistance (Guido laughing)
- **End:** Full rebellion, liberation from abuser

### Deleted Content Evolution
- **Original concept:** Ciccio was going to be another sea monster friend of Luca and Alberto
- **Change reason:** Detracted from main friendship focus
- **Final version:** Better serves as example of toxic friendship vs. healthy (Luca/Alberto)

## Abilities & Skills (Limited)

### Ciccio's Skills
- **Swimming:** Competes in race (with cheating aid)
- **Physical strength:** Can catch/hold Vespa
- **Endurance:** Takes physical abuse without complaint

### Guido's Skills
- **Boat operation:** Helps steer Ercole's speedboat
- **Pasta eating:** Forced to compete, not naturally skilled
- **Slight independence:** Can resist Ercole's worst impulses

### Shared Limitations
- **No real leadership:** Follow rather than lead
- **Easily manipulated:** Controlled through intimidation
- **Poor judgment:** Don't recognize abuse until extreme

## Trivia & Details

### Names
- **Guido:** Shares name with *Cars* character (unrelated)
- **Ciccio:** Italian diminutive name (means "chubby" informally)

### Character Design
- **Contrasting builds:** Ciccio round, Guido thin (visual distinction)
- **Matching role:** Both wear striped shirts (parallel to each other)
- **Color coding:** Different color schemes for easy identification

### Development
- **Original Ciccio concept:** Was going to be third sea monster friend
- **Why changed:** Filmmakers wanted focus on Luca-Alberto friendship
- **Final role:** Better serves as cautionary example of bad friendship

### Not Appearing In
- **Ciao Alberto:** Neither mentioned nor appears (focused on Alberto-Massimo)
